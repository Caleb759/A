PESTS

And the problems they pose:

Pests, such as insects and rodents, interfere with human activities by damaging crops, spreading disease, or invading homes and buildings. They can pose serious health risks, harm food supplies, and cause significant economic losses. Managing pests often involves a combination of prevention, control, and elimination methods, but improper use of chemicals or lack of sustainable practices can lead to environmental damage.
PESTS PROBLEMS SOLUTIONS
Pests are very dangerous and harmful to the environment as they can significantly disrupt ecosystems, compete with native species for resources, and even cause declines in native populations. They can cause many problems such as structural damage to buildings, contamination of food and surfaces, allergic reactions, and the spread of diseases. We can stop pests destroying the environment by maintaining a clean environment, using physical barriers and traps, attracting beneficial insects, and employing natural or chemical pest control methods as needed.
